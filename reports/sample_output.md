## Business Insights

- A small number of products drive the majority of revenue (Office Chairs dominate sales)
- Repeat customers, while fewer, contribute significantly to revenue
- Average Order Value is relatively high, indicating strong pricing power
- Furniture and lighting categories perform best

---

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-performing products (Office Chairs, Lamps)
- Introduce retention strategies (email marketing, loyalty programs)
- Bundle complementary products to increase AOV further
- Reduce reliance on discounts to protect margins

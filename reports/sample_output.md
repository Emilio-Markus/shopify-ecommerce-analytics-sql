# 📊 E-commerce Data Analysis Report

## 🧾 Executive Summary

This report analyzes e-commerce transaction data to evaluate revenue performance, customer behavior, and product trends.

The goal is to identify key revenue drivers and opportunities for growth.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** $2,090  
- **Total Orders:** 10  
- **Total Customers:** 7  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $209  

---

## 📅 Revenue Trend

| Month   | Revenue | Orders |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 2024-01 | $1,550 | 7 |
| 2024-02 | $540   | 3 |

### Insight

Revenue decreased significantly from January to February.

This may indicate:
- Seasonal demand changes  
- Reduced customer activity  
- Marketing or pricing impact  

---

## 🛒 Product Performance

| Product        | Revenue |
|----------------|---------|
| Office Chair   | $760    |
| Modern Lamp    | $440    |
| Coffee Table   | $300    |
| Rug            | $270    |
| Wall Art       | $230    |
| Desk Lamp      | $90     |

### Insight

Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing products.

This suggests:
- Strong product-market fit for top items  
- Opportunity to scale winning products  

---

## 👥 Customer Behavior

- Repeat Customers: 2  
- One-Time Customers: 5  

### Insight

The majority of customers are one-time buyers.

This indicates:
- Weak customer retention  
- Opportunity to improve lifecycle marketing  

---

## 📌 Key Findings

- Revenue is heavily concentrated in a small number of products  
- Customer retention is low  
- Monthly performance fluctuates significantly  
- Furniture and lighting categories perform best  

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

### 1. Focus on High-Performing Products
Increase marketing spend on top products such as Office Chairs and Modern Lamps.

### 2. Improve Customer Retention
Introduce:
- Email campaigns  
- Loyalty incentives  
- Retargeting ads  

### 3. Increase Average Order Value
Bundle complementary products together.

### 4. Analyze Revenue Decline
Investigate why February revenue dropped.

### 5. Expand Strong Categories
Invest in Furniture and Lighting categories.

---

## 📊 Conclusion

This analysis highlights key opportunities to improve revenue and customer retention.

By focusing on top-performing products and strengthening customer engagement, the business can drive sustainable growth.
